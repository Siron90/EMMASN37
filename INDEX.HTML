About ForexPro

Learn about our mission, values, and commitment to traders

Our Story

ForexPro was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to make forex trading accessible to everyone while providing the tools and education needed to trade successfully. Over the years, we've grown to serve traders in over 100 countries.

Our Values

  • Transparency: We believe in clear pricing and honest communication with our clients.
  • Education: We're committed to providing comprehensive educational resources for traders of all levels.
  • Innovation: We continuously develop new tools and features to enhance the trading experience.
  • Security: Client funds and data protection are our top priorities.

Regulation & Security

ForexPro is regulated by multiple financial authorities worldwide. We maintain segregated client accounts, use advanced encryption technology, and undergo regular audits to ensure the highest standards of security and compliance.
